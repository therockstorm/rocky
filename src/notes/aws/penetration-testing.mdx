title: Penetration Testing
date: "2022-11-21"
---

- Approved services without permission ([up-to-date list](https://aws.amazon.com/security/penetration-testing/))
  - EC2 including NAT Gateway and ELBs
  - RDS
  - CloudFront
  - Aurora
  - API Gateway
  - Lambda and Lambda Edge
  - Lightsail
  - Elastic Beanstalk
- Certain activities are prohibited even with approved services
- To avoid service disruption, recommended to avoid T3/T2 nano, T1 micro, and M1 small
